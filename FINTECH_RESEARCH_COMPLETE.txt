SMARTMONEY COMPETITIVE ADVANTAGE
================================================================================

UNIQUE POSITIONING: Privacy + Self-Hosted + Personalization

Hard to Copy (High Barrier):
  ✓ Self-hosted PWA architecture (tech complexity, DevOps)
  ✓ Privacy-first design (requires full rearchitect for competitors)
  ✓ AI trained on user's own data (data network effect)
  ✓ Household collaboration UX (complex permission system)
  ✓ Regional market expertise (Japan, Vietnam localization)

Easy to Copy (Low Barrier):
  • Visualizations (heatmaps, charts) — mitigate via interactivity
  • Open Banking APIs (Plaid SDK) — mitigate via UX differentiation
  • Receipt OCR — mitigate via AI categorization accuracy
