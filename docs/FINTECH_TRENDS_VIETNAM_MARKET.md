## Market Overview

- **Key Players:** Zalopay, Momo, GCash (wallets), no dominant PFM app
- **Economy Type:** Digital-first, mobile-first, limited traditional banking
- **Dominant Use Case:** Digital payments (76.63% of market)
- **Culture:** Remittance-heavy, family money management, informal savings
- **Key Metric:** QR adoption +106% YoY in 2024 (fastest global)
- **Regulatory:** Decree 94/2025 (sandbox), Law 71/2025 (crypto recognition Jan 1, 2026)

---

## Top Opportunities

### 1. QR Payment Auto-Import
**Benefit:** Captures fastest-growing payment type (VietQR, not card-based)
**Revenue:** Premium tier feature
**Difficulty:** Low-Medium | **Timeline:** 3-4 months

### 2. Remittance Tracking + Family Accounts
**Benefit:** Major use case (remittances = 7% of GDP, OFW families)
**Revenue:** Premium feature + remittance provider partnerships
**Difficulty:** Medium | **Timeline:** 4-6 months

### 3. Multi-Currency + Crypto Portfolio
**Benefit:** Aligns with Law 71/2025 (crypto legitimacy) + remittance patterns (VND/USD)
**Revenue:** Premium tier, crypto partnership integrations
**Difficulty:** Medium | **Timeline:** 4-5 months

### 4. SME Accounting Add-On
**Benefit:** Market pivoting from consumer to SME lending (underserved)
**Revenue:** Premium SME tier (separate pricing from personal finance)
**Difficulty:** Medium-High | **Timeline:** 6-8 months (research + build)

### 5. Zalopay / Momo Integration
**Benefit:** Covers ~80% of digital payment use cases
**Revenue:** White-label partnerships with wallet providers
**Difficulty:** High | **Timeline:** 9-12 months (requires provider APIs)
