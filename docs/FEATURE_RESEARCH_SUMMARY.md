## Quick Wins for SmartMoney (Next Sprint)

### High Impact, Low Effort
1. **CSV/JSON Export API** ‚Äî Data portability differentiator; attracts self-hosted users
2. **Spending Threshold Alerts** ‚Äî Simple notification rules; behavior change enabler
3. **Bill Splitting Module** ‚Äî Template-based simple splitting; group expenses
4. **Rewards Category Tags** ‚Äî Let users track credit card earning/redemption manually
5. **Goal Templates** ‚Äî Emergency fund, vacation, home; tie to budget categories

### High Impact, Medium Effort
1. **Receipt OCR Integration** ‚Äî Partner with Google Vision or Tesseract; Japanese/Vietnamese models
2. **Multi-User Shared Budgets** ‚Äî Role-based access (viewer, editor, admin); offline sync handling
3. **Automation Rules Engine** ‚Äî Trigger-based rules (if category > limit, then notify)
4. **Portfolio Tracking** ‚Äî Manual input for stocks/bonds; unify with DeFi tracking
5. **Cash Flow Forecasting** ‚Äî Linear model; recurring transaction patterns

### High Impact, High Effort
1. **Bank API Integrations** ‚Äî Japan (Rakuten, MUFG, SBI) + Vietnam (Vietcombank, BIDV)
2. **Advanced OCR Model** ‚Äî Train on Japanese receipts + Vietnamese transaction data
3. **Behavioral ML** ‚Äî Anomaly detection; spending pattern analysis; habit tracking

## Regional Expansion Priorities

### Japan Market (High Monetization)
**Top 5 Must-Haves:**
1. Bank integration (Rakuten, MUFG, SBI)
2. Kanji-capable auto-categorization
3. Rewards/cashback tracking (complex JP card programs)
4. Japanese OCR for receipts
5. Mobile-first experience

**Market Context:** Tech-savvy users; strong privacy concerns; reward shopping culture; salary-based income; couple finances management

### Vietnam Market (High Growth)
**Top 5 Must-Haves:**
1. Mobile payment integration (MoMo, ZaloPay, VietQR)
2. Vietnamese auto-categorization (diacritics handling)
3. Bank sync (15+ major banks)
4. Offline capability (connectivity gaps)
5. Low data usage (mobile plan constraints)

**Market Context:** Mobile-only users; irregular income; group expense sharing; limited credit card adoption; QR code payment growth
