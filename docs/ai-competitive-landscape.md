## Key Differentiators

### Copilot Money (Apple Only, Strongest AI)
- **Strengths:** Best categorization accuracy (2 weeks to learn patterns), forecasting, chat
- **Limitation:** iOS only (critical for SMB market)
- **Privacy:** Cloud-based (OpenAI, Apple APIs)
- **Gap:** No web version; couples to Apple ecosystem

### Monarch Money (Balanced, Family-Focused)
- **Strengths:** t-GNN forecasting (best-in-class), family budgeting, multi-platform
- **Limitation:** Fewer customization options
- **Privacy:** Cloud-based
- **Gap:** Weaker on mobile UX vs. web

### YNAB (Philosophy-Based, Non-AI)
- **Strengths:** Zero-based budgeting, strong community, educational content
- **Limitation:** Less AI, requires discipline + manual categorization
- **Privacy:** Plaid-dependent (aggregation)
- **Gap:** Doesn't learn from user behavior

### Cleo (Behavioral Coaching)
- **Strengths:** AI coaching, personality-driven, engaging tone
- **Limitation:** Less emphasis on forecasting, budgeting secondary
- **Privacy:** Cloud-based
- **Gap:** Not a complete budgeting solution
